Privacy policy

Your support should not cost your privacy.

This site collects no personal information, runs no analytics, and keeps no supporter database. Your pledge is recorded only in your own browser. Here is exactly what that means.

Updated June 2026

Personal information

The site has no form for your name, email, address, or payment information. The pledge is not submitted to a government agency or third-party petition platform.

Browser storage

When you press the pledge button, the site saves a single flag in your browser's local storage indicating that this browser has pledged support. Nothing is transmitted anywhere.

Clearing the data

You can remove the stored flag by clearing site data for this domain in your browser. It is not synchronized between devices.

Third parties

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and links to external data sources. Those services have their own privacy practices. No analytics or tracking script is included here.

Security

Because no personal pledge data is collected by this site, there is no supporter database containing personal information to breach.

Independence

Pave Over MoCo is an independent advocacy project. It is not affiliated with Montgomery County government or any of the agencies and research organizations cited on the policy pages.
