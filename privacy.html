Privacy policy

Your support should not cost your privacy.

This site currently records a support pledge only in your own browser. Here is exactly what that means.

Personal information

The site has no form for your name, email, address, or payment information. The current pledge is not submitted to a government agency or third-party petition platform.

Browser storage

When you press the pledge button, the site saves whether that browser has pledged support using local storage.

Clearing the data

You can remove those values by clearing site data for this domain in your browser. They are not synchronized between devices.

Third parties

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and links to external sources. Those services have their own privacy practices. No analytics script is included here.

Security

Because no personal pledge data is collected by this site, there is currently no supporter database containing personal information.

Independence

Pave Over MoCo is an independent advocacy project and is not affiliated with Montgomery County government or the external publications and agencies linked on the policy pages.
