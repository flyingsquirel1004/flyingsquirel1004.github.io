Privacy policy

We do not need your data. We need your lane.

The petition is a browser-local joke. Here is the less amusing, accurate explanation of what the site stores.

Personal information

The site has no form for your name, email, address, or payment information. It does not submit a petition to any organization.

Browser storage

When you press the petition button, two values are saved in your browser's local storage: a ceremonial count and whether that browser has already signed.

Clearing the data

You can remove those values by clearing site data for this domain in your browser. They are not synchronized between devices.

Third parties

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and links to external sources. Those services have their own privacy practices. No analytics script is included here.

Security

Because no personal petition data is collected by this site, there is no petition database to secure, sell, lose, or accidentally email to a highway contractor.

Satire disclosure

Pave Over MoCo is satire and is not affiliated with Montgomery County, its agencies, or the external publications and agencies linked on the policy pages.
