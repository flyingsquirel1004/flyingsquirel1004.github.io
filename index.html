The three-part platform

A transportation agenda for drivers.

Most county households rely on roads for work, school, shopping, caregiving, and emergencies. Our platform asks transportation leaders to treat road capacity as essential public infrastructure.

POLICY 01

Expand roads where demand exceeds capacity.

Target recurring bottlenecks, improve intersections, and widen critical corridors where analysis supports it.

Read the road brief
POLICY 02

Stop removing lanes without accountability.

Require transparent traffic analysis before converting travel lanes or parking into lightly used bike facilities.

Review bike-lane impacts
POLICY 03

Fund transit based on measurable results.

Protect general traffic lanes and direct limited transportation dollars toward services people consistently use.

Review bus-lane impacts
Road space is public infrastructure. Its allocation deserves public scrutiny.

Our approach

Practical policy. Transparent tradeoffs.

Transportation decisions create winners and losers. We support evaluating projects with real traffic data, clear costs, and direct attention to the residents and businesses that depend on vehicle access.

  • 01Measure travel time and congestion before and after projects.
  • 02Publish the cost and capacity impact of every lane conversion.
  • 03Give drivers a meaningful voice before changes become permanent.
